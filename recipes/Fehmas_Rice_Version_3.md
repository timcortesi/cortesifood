This is Fehma's rice recipe that has
chicken in it ;-) Serve with 
[Chicken Dish, Version 2](Chicken_Dish_Version_2) and Fehma's 
[Potatoes & Eggplant](Potatoes_&_Eggplant) dish.

-   2 1/2 cups basmati brown rice
-   1 onion, cut against the grain into long, thin slices
-   Olive oil
-   3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
-   1/2" ginger root, thinly sliced in 2 directions
-   1/2 of a boneless skinless chicken breast, cut into 1/2" pieces
-   1 1/2 teaspoon salt
-   2 1/2 teaspoon meat masala
-   1/2 small long green chili peppers, diced
-   3/4 teaspoon garam masala

Cover rice with water â€“ let soak 15-30 min. Meanwhile, saute onion in
oil in bottom of 6 qt. Dutch oven. Add garlic and ginger. Add uncooked
chicken, 6 cups water, salt, meat masala, and diced chili pepper. Drain
rice and add to pot along with garam masala. Simmer until all water is
absorbed.

 

Makes abt 8 cups of rice!
