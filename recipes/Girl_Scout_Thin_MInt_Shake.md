Really good if you like Thin Mint Girl Scout cookies.

-   3/4 cup milk
-   3 large scoops vanilla ice cream
-   2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
-   6 Thin Mint cookies

In a blender, combine milk, vanilla ice cream, chocolate syrup and
cookies. Enjoy.

Makes 2 shakes.
