 Excellent on a cold winter day.

-   1 gallon Wilkens apple cider
-   2 tablespoons honey
-   1 tablespoon lemon juice
-   1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
-   1/4 teaspoon cloves

Mix all ingredients and simmer. Enjoy with a warm cider donut.
